Refund Policy

Your satisfaction is our priority. Therefore, all eligible courses purchased on GR Networks can be refunded within 7 days. If, for any reason, you're not satisfied with a course, you can request a refund as long as it aligns with our refund policy guidelines. Please note that some restrictions may apply, and certain purchases may only be eligible for credit refunds. For further details on our refund policy, please refer to the information below.

While our 7-day refund policy is designed to give students a risk-free learning experience, we must also safeguard our instructors from fraud and ensure a fair payment schedule. Therefore, refund requests made after the refund window will not be processed.

The refund will be issued to the original payment method used for purchasing the course.

We reserve the right to limit or deny refund requests at our discretion in cases of suspected refund abuse, including but not limited to the following:

  • A significant portion of the course has been consumed or downloaded by a student before the refund was requested.

  • Multiple refunds have been requested by a student for the same course.

  • Excessive refunds have been requested by a student.

Users who have their account banned or course access disabled due to a violation of our Terms will not be eligible to receive a refund. We do not grant refunds for any subscription services after 7 days. These refund restrictions will be enforced to the extent permitted by applicable law.

As the payment for the bundle was processed by the third-party vendor, we do not have access to the transaction details and are unable to process a refund. Please reach out to the third-party vendor directly to request a refund.
